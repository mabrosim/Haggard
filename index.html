Welcome to Haggard home!

Haggard is a web-based task board with support of agile methods like Scrum or Kanban. To try out the board, please navigate to Demo Board and log in with Guest account!

Made in Oulu with Passion!

Inspired by Kanban core principle of work visualization, together with my great colleague, Heikki Hellgren (@drodil), we have developed Haggard in 2012-2014 as hobby project. It attracted almost 1000 users and was used daily to support agile practices within company.
